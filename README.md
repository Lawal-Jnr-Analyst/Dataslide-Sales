# 📊 Dataslide Sales Dashboard Analysis


## 📊 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of Dataslide sales performance across multiple regions and time periods. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights, focusing on revenue trends, regional performance, and top-performing companies by employee ID.
The goal is to help stakeholders identify high-performing regions, optimize sales strategies based on company contribution, and understand revenue distribution across different years and geographic areas.

The goal is to help stakeholders identify high-performing regions, optimize inventory based on product popularity, and understand the effectiveness of different sales channels.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- PowerBi
- Power Query
- DAX

  
## 💡 Data Insights
- A significant surge in revenue was recorded in 1997, reaching over 60M compared to approximately 20M in 1996, suggesting successful business expansion or increased market demand during that period.
- The Eastern region dominates revenue contribution at 72.65M, accounting for the largest share of total revenue compared to Southern (22.94M), Western (21.58M), and Northern (19.07M) regions.
- Save-a-lot Markets leads as the top revenue-generating company, followed closely by Ernst Handel and QUICK-Stop, highlighting a concentrated dependency on a few key accounts that present strong partnership opportunities.
